About UKmandu

UKmandu is a community-driven platform dedicated to discovering and promoting Nepali events across the United Kingdom.

Founded and managed by Dev Neupane and Pradeep Chhetri, UKmandu aims to create a trusted central hub where event organisers and the Nepali community can connect easily.

From concerts and festivals to cultural nights and community gatherings, UKmandu brings all Nepali events in the UK into one place.
