ABOUT UKMANDU

A media platform built for the Nepali community in the UK

UKmandu is a UK-based community media platform sharing honest conversations, inspiring journeys and practical information for Nepalese people living in or planning to come to the United Kingdom.

Through our podcast, event promotion and trusted business partnerships, we connect people, share useful experiences and help strengthen the Nepali community.
